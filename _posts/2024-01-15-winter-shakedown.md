The first laps of the year are always a little cautious. <!--more-->Cold tires, cold brakes, and a track layered with grit make every corner an experiment. I run short stints, log temps, and lean on data to guide the tweaks.

The reward arrives when the chassis settles into rhythm and the telemetry finally draws the smooth arcs I imagined in the garage.
